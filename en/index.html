Live in 4 steps

Your cashback structure can be active within about 15 minutes.

1

Share your Google Ads customer ID

You provide the customer ID of your Google Ads account. We do not change campaigns, budgets, or settings. We only connect through the official Google structure.

2

Account validation

We send an invite that you accept in Google Ads. Then we validate whether the account meets the program requirements. This is a one time check and has no impact on performance or running campaigns.

3

Billing profile switch

After validation, the billing profile is switched to our MCC billing profile. From that moment, Google Ads charges run through our profile. This is required to apply cashback and remains fully transparent.

4

Monthly payout

On the first day of each month, your verified cashback is paid out in euros. You see the exact amount in advance via your dashboard, based on verified Google Ads spend. No manual work, no surprises.

Frequently asked questions

Is this allowed by Google? expand_more

Yes. We operate in line with official Google Ads API policies and recognized partner programs. Sandstone is a certified Google Partner.

How do I receive cashback on my Google Ads spend? expand_more

After linking and validation, billing for Google Ads runs through our MCC billing profile. Based on verified spend, you receive monthly cashback in euros.

How much cashback can I receive? expand_more

Depending on your daily Google Ads budget, you can receive up to 5% cashback. Higher spend means a higher cashback rate.

Does this change my campaigns or performance? expand_more

No. Your campaigns, settings, bidding, and optimizations remain unchanged. We only validate and handle billing for cashback purposes.

Do we keep full control of our Google Ads account? expand_more

Yes. You remain the owner and keep full control. We do not require management access to campaigns or bidding strategies.

Does this affect my data or the Google algorithm? expand_more

No. There is no impact on account data, learning phases, or algorithms. The change is administrative and financial only. Billing profile switches are scheduled during off hours to minimize downtime.

How do invoicing and payments work? expand_more

Billing follows a semi postpaid model, similar to Google Ads. You first generate spend in your account. Once a threshold is reached, an invoice is issued and needs to be paid.

This keeps your spending controlled and transparent. Spend remains visible and capped. All transactions follow the standard Google Ads billing flow.

Cashback is calculated on verified monthly spend and paid out in euros to your bank account each month.

When is cashback paid out? expand_more

On the first day of each month you receive cashback on the verified spend from the previous month.

What if I want to stop? expand_more

You can stop according to the agreement terms. You always keep the right to cashback that has already been accrued and verified.

Who is this program for? expand_more

Advertisers with consistent Google Ads spend who want transparent savings on an existing budget, without operational changes.
